Deloitte Start Up Competition (Irvine, CA)

UCI partnered with Deloitte for this two day competition where students where given the following problem statement “In what ways through technology can the UCI Campus be improved” following idea pitch submissions, students where placed into teams where they had a chance to work with a Deloitte Consultant and further develop their idea and pitch it to Deloitte partners and UCI Faculty the following day.
